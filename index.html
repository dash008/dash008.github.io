I am interested in the place of video games in contemporary art, this interest has led me to create artworks that explore social criticism using aesthetics, mechanics and video game technologies as platform.

PANDEMIC SURVIVAL SIMULATOR

Pandemic Survival Simulator is a video game that aims to put the player in a tight spot by managing a street vendor in the middle of a quarantine

PROJECT NITRO

A game simulation of a pilot driver in a car with crazy drift

DEMOCRACY GUARDIAN SIMULATOR

A Videogame Installation focused in a state policy

HG-S34L

A game made by hacking the Timberman code to reframe the semantics of the original game

#RECICLEMOSCONTOTO

Simple platformer game focusing in environmental issues in Argentina

SHRADDHA

Shraddha comes from the concept of Samsara in Buddhism, an endless cycle of constant transformation
